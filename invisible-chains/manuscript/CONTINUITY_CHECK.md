## CONTINUITY ISSUE #1: Timeline Overlap
**Issue**: Chapters 12-14 describe a 5-6 month expansion period, but Chapter 15 says "6 months after Chapter 10" which would place it around the same time as Chapter 12.

**Resolution**: This appears intentional - Elara arrives DURING the expansion period, not after. The timeline makes sense if:
- Ch10: Decision to build (month 2)
- Ch11: First building (month 4)
- Ch12: 7 buildings (month 9)
- Ch15: Elara arrives (month 8-9, during the expansion)
- Ch13-14: Concurrent with Ch15-16
- Ch17-20: Months 9-13

**Status**: NOT AN ERROR - parallel storylines converging

## CONTINUITY ISSUE #2: Money Math
**Issue**: Chapter 19 states 2.5 billion spent over 18 months, but documented sales only show ~650 million.

**Resolution**: The Collector makes ongoing purchases that aren't all documented in detail. Jax has "billions more in unsold currency" and keeps selling batches. The math works if we assume he's sold 3-4 billion total worth of currency to The Collector across the 18-month period.

**Status**: MINOR - Could be clarified but plausible with ongoing sales

## Building Count Progression
- Ch 11: 1 building (Building 47, Sector 4), 20 units, 165 total capacity
- Ch 12: 7 buildings total, 165 units, 640 residents
- Ch 14: 19 buildings, 3,500-4,500 residents
- Ch 19 report: 19 buildings, 4,500 residents (4,200 Gamma, 300 Beta)

## CONTINUITY ISSUE #3: Unit Count Discrepancy
**Issue**: Chapter 11 states 20 units in Building 47, Chapter 12 states 165 units total across 7 buildings (average 23.5 units per building), but then doesn't track units for the 19-building expansion.

**Resolution**: The 165 figure in Ch11 might be an error. If Building 47 has 20 units, and 7 buildings total have 165 units, that's ~23 units average which is consistent. However, Ch11 says "165 total capacity" which might mean occupancy, not units.

**Status**: MINOR INCONSISTENCY - Ch11 uses "165 units" and "165 total capacity" interchangeably; likely means "space for 165 people" across 7 buildings with varying unit counts.
